What happens to your body
over time

🦠

Gut Microbiome Damage

Emulsifiers like Polysorbate 80 erode the intestinal mucus layer, promoting inflammation and dysbiosis — the root of many chronic diseases.

🧠

Neurological Effects

Artificial colors (Red 40, Yellow 5) and sodium benzoate are associated with hyperactivity, ADHD, and behavioral changes, especially in children.

🩺

Cancer Risk

BHA, BHT, sodium nitrate, and caramel color IV are classified as possible or probable carcinogens by major international health organizations.

⚖️

Metabolic Disorders

High fructose corn syrup impairs insulin sensitivity and liver function, contributing to metabolic syndrome and type 2 diabetes.
