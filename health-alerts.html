Safety Reminders

🏥 General Food Safety
Best Practices

📖

Always Read Labels

Check ingredient lists even on products labeled "natural" or "healthy." Marketing claims aren't regulated by the FDA.

🍽️

Watch Portion Sizes

Nutrition facts are per serving, but packages often contain 2–3 servings. Multiply all values accordingly.

🌿

Choose Whole Foods

Products with fewer than 5 recognizable ingredients are generally safer. If you can't read it, reconsider it.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Foods high in sodium (>600mg/serving) require extra water intake to support proper kidney processing of excess salt.
