Smart Shopping

🛒 Tips for cleaner grocery shopping

📋
5-Ingredient Rule
Choose products with 5 or fewer recognizable ingredients. If you need a chemistry degree to read it, skip it.
🔤
Order Matters
Ingredients are listed by weight. Sugar listed first? That's the main ingredient — full stop.
🏪
Shop the Perimeter
Fresh produce, dairy, and proteins are on the store edges. Ultra-processed products fill the inner aisles.
🌿
Pronounce It Test
If you cannot pronounce an ingredient naturally, your body probably wasn't designed to process it efficiently.
